Payment Security FAQs

Are my card details stored by Shree Prakash Genral? expand_more
No. We do not store any credit/debit card information on our servers. All card details are processed through encrypted channels directly to our banking partners who are PCI-DSS certified.
What happens if a transaction fails? expand_more
In case of a failed transaction where money has been deducted, the amount is usually automatically reversed to your account within 5-7 business days by your bank. Our support team is always available to help track such cases.
Is it safe to use UPI for large transactions? expand_more
Absolutely. UPI transactions are backed by NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India) and require a secure MPIN. They are as safe as traditional banking methods for any amount within your bank's daily limit.
How do I know the payment page is secure? expand_more
Look for the padlock icon in your browser's address bar and ensure the URL begins with "https://". This indicates an active SSL connection protecting your data.
