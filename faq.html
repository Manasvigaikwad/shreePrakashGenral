Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your order by clicking on the "Track Order" link in your confirmation email or by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. Once your order has shipped, we will provide a tracking number that you can use on our shipping partner's website for real-time updates.

info

Standard processing time is 1-2 business days before shipping.

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy for most items. Products must be in their original packaging and unused condition. To start a return, visit our Return Center and enter your order number. Please note that certain items like perishable goods or personalized items are not eligible for return.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For select regions, we also offer local payment methods and installment options via Klarna or Afterpay.

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placement. After this window, our warehouse typically begins processing, and changes may not be possible. Please contact our support team immediately if you need to make urgent changes.
